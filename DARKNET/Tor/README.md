# Tor

## Overview

The Onion Router. Free software for anonymous communication. Routes traffic through multiple encrypted relays.

## Era

2002-present

## What Makes It Interesting

Layered encryption (onion routing). Hidden services (.onion sites). The gold standard for online anonymity. Used by journalists, activists, and privacy-conscious users worldwide.

## Revival Potential

**High**

## Current Status

Alive. Gold standard for anonymity. Actively developed and maintained by Tor Project.
