# WebFinger

## Overview

RFC 7033. Protocol for discovering information about people and resources based on email addresses or URLs.

## Era

2010s

## What Makes It Interesting

Links identity across services. Federated social networking foundation. Used by Mastodon/ActivityPub ecosystem. Simple JSON-based responses. Enables 'Sign in with' flows across federated services.

## Revival Potential

**High**

## Current Status

Alive. Used by Mastodon and ActivityPub ecosystem. Growing importance for federated identity.
