# Sametime

## Overview

IBM/Lotus Sametime enterprise instant messaging protocol. Integration with Lotus Notes/Domino suite. Presence detection across enterprise applications.

## Era

1998-present

## What Makes It Interesting

Enterprise-grade presence system. Meeting scheduling baked into the protocol. Could detect presence in documents, not just people.

## Revival Potential

**Low**

## Current Status

Alive under HCL (acquired from IBM). Enterprise-locked.
