# Aido

## Overview

Sega's online gaming protocol for the Dreamcast console. Enabled early console online gaming.

## Era

1990s

## What Makes It Interesting

Early console online gaming. SegaNet service. Phantasy Star Online was the killer app. Proved console online gaming was viable.

## Revival Potential

**Low**

## Current Status

Dead. Console-specific.
