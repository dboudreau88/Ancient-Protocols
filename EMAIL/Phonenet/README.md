# Phonenet

## Overview

CSNET's dial-up mail relay protocol. Provided email access to machines without ARPANET connectivity using phone lines.

## Era

1981

## What Makes It Interesting

Store-and-forward over phone lines. Bridged the gap between ARPANET-connected machines and the rest. Enabled email for universities and research institutions that couldn't afford ARPANET connections.

## Revival Potential

**Low**

## Current Status

Dead. Historical significance only.
