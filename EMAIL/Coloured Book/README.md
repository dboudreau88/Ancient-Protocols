# Coloured Book

## Overview

UK academic email protocol suite (JNT Mail, Grey Book, etc.). Used on UK academic networks like JANET.

## Era

1975-1992

## What Makes It Interesting

Ran on UK academic networks. Colour-coded naming convention (Yellow Book, Grey Book, etc.). Pre-Internet email standards that served UK academia for nearly two decades.

## Revival Potential

**Low**

## Current Status

Dead. Historical only.
