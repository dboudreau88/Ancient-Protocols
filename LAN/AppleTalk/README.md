# AppleTalk

## Overview

Apple's LAN protocol suite. Enabled zero-configuration networking for Mac computers. Auto-addressing, auto-discovery, and file/print sharing.

## Era

1985-2000s

## What Makes It Interesting

Zero-configuration networking before it was a buzzword. Auto-addressing. Every Mac could share files and printers automatically. Ideas live on in Bonjour/mDNS.

## Revival Potential

**Medium**

## Current Status

Dead. Concepts live on in Bonjour/mDNS. Historical significance.
