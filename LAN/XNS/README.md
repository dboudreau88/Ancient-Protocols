# XNS

## Overview

Xerox Network Systems. Developed at Xerox PARC. Influenced TCP/IP, IPX, and AppleTalk.

## Era

1981

## What Makes It Interesting

Influenced TCP/IP, IPX, and AppleTalk. Datagram + sequenced packet model. Ethernet was developed alongside XNS. The grandfather of modern networking protocols.

## Revival Potential

**Low**

## Current Status

Dead. Historical only.
